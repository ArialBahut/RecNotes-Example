hover notes
When you move the cursor over different elements of the notes, some elements will highlight and allow you to edit them by a mouse click.
  1. topic titles
  2. create a new note
  3. edit event properties (time, tags)
  4. edit the text of a note
  5. edit a link
  6. follow a link
In addition to these, when you are looking at a sub-topic, you can click outside of the above to focus/select the sub-topic.
